Patricia Realini is a Cultural Event Coordinator and cofounder of 5 North Event Design Group, crafting events and community based marketing strategies for nonprofits and businesses in Anaheim, CA. Her work in Anaheim includes Downtown Anaheim's Art Crawl Experience, Anaheim Neighborhood Association's Home, Garden & Brewery Tour & Anaheim Brewery's Oktoberfest. Previously Patricia has headed up the event calendar for LAB Holdings (The Lab & The Camp shopping centers in Costa Mesa, CA) and hosted fundraisers, movie nights, concerts, festivals & private parties. In 2013, Patricia was honored by Tom Tait, Mayor of Anaheim, & the Anaheim City Council "as a founder and creator of the successful Art Crawl Experience, bringing arts and culture to the Downtown Anaheim Arts District through a unique visual and auditory art walk and arts festival". A recent graduate of Chapman University's Argyros School of Business, Patricia holds a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration with an emphasis in Marketing. Her expertise centers on community interaction & social connectivity online and in person. In her spare time she likes to camp in Big Sur & bake cheesecakes.
