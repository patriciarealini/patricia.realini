PATRICIA REALINI

Patricia Realini is a software engineer, an artist and a woman in Los Angeles, CA. Currently she works at Laurel + Wolf, an interior design startup, with React, Sass and Rails. Patricia is also an active member of the programming community. In 2015 she was invited to attend the JQuery Developer Summit where she contributed to npm and mentored new comers to open source. As a member of MaptimeLA — the Los Angeles chapter of Maptime's community of geographers & coders who contribute to Open Street Maps & GIS development — Patricia led a workshop on Git & GitHub called Get Git Got, an introduction to Git through the command line. With MaptimeLA, Patricia attended HackForLA in June 2015. At HackForLA, a civic minded hackathon, she helped create Pool Party, a web app for connecting commuters.
